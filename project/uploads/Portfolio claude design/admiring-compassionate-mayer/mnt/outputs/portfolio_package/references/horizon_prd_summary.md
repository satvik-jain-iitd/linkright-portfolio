## What it is
A full Product Requirements Document (PRD) for a gamified stock market app targeting new retail investors in India. Co-authored by Satvik Jain as part of the GGI Impact Fellowship 2024.

## Key product details
- **Problem:** 63% of new investors feel overwhelmed by market volatility. Traditional platforms don't educate or engage.
- **Solution:** Gamified stock trading simulation using real NSE/BSE data with virtual currency, AI-driven behavioral analytics, leaderboards, and structured learning modules.
- **Target users:** New retail investors aged 25-40 in India (154M demat accounts by April 2024).
- **Core features:** Simulated trading, daily challenges, AI insights, social trading, broker integration
- **Tech stack defined:** Node.js, Python, TensorFlow, Apache Kafka, TradingView charting, MongoDB
- **Business model:** Freemium with premium educational content and advanced analytics
